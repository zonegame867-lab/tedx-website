Our Manifesto

Where illusion meets insight. TEDx Silver Oaks is a stage where the brightest minds gather to share stories, ideas, and visions that challenge perception and reveal deeper truths. Maya is not just a theme — it’s a journey into the unseen.

Theme: Maya

Maya, in ancient philosophy, represents illusion — the veil that obscures reality. At TEDx Silver Oaks, we explore how perception shapes truth, how stories distort or reveal, and how breaking illusions can lead to transformation.
